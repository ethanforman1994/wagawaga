How it works
Walk around your neighborhood and collect glowing dots. Dots are placed at real intersections near you. Golden dots are rare and worth 25 points each. Dots reset every midnight so there's always a reason to walk tomorrow.
Beta notes
This is an early beta. Leaderboards and friend competition are coming in the next update. Your scores are saved locally on your device for now. Share feedback to beta@wagawaga.app
